Baking instructions for digiorno pizza

  • Preheat the oven to 400°F or 200°F.
  • Keep the pizza frozen while preheating.
  • Remove the pizza from the freshness wrap and discard the plastic wrap.
  • Place the pizza directly on the center oven rack.
  • for 18 to 21 minutes at 400°F or 22 to 25 minutes at 200°F.
